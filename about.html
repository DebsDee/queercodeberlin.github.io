Non-Profit

This group has no commercial interests whatsoever. We're against pink washing anything. We are here to find new friends, colleagues, mentors. We want to be with people like us.

What We Consider Queer

It's as easy as it gets: People who identify as queer. This might include zero, one or more of: Agender, Ally, Androgyne, Asexual, Bicurious, Bigender, Bisexual, Demiboy, Demigirl, Demiguy, Elissogender, Enby, Feminine of Center, Gay, Gender Non-Conforming, Genderfluid, Genderflux, Genderqueer, Homosexual, Intergender, Intersexual, Lesbian, Masculine of Center, Maverique, Multigender, Multisexual, Neutrois, Non-Binary, Non-Monosexual, Novosexual, Pangender, Pansexual, Pansexual, Polygender, Polysexual, Queer, Questioning, Skoliosexual, Trans*, Transgender, Transsexual, Trigender.
